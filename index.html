How to play :
  • Cards
    • There is a total of 21 cards.
    • 3 of these cards are part of the solution to this crime.
    • One suspect, one location, one weapon.
    • The remaining 18 cards are evenly distributed among the players.
    • These 18 cards is NOT part of the solution.
    • In this game you play with the AI Bart and Liza.
  • Roll Dice
    • Rolls over 3 -> Move to another room.
    • You may now guess your oponents cards based on what you filled in
      in the drop down fields.
    • Rolls below 3 -> You are stuck.
    • Simply end the turn by rolling the dice again.
  • Guess
    • You always have to guess the cards of your opponent to your left.
    • Once all their cards are revealed you can continue to guess your next
      opponents cards (clockwise).
    • You can only guess once per turn.
  • Accuse
    • Accuse is your final solution, and uses the same drop down field as Guess.
    • An accusation will end the game and reveal the truth.
    • You can always make an accusation, no matter what you roll.
